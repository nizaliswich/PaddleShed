About the PaddleShed Family

Established in January 2012, PaddleShed started as a single gym situated in Bedok Reservoir Park.
Over the years, PaddleShed has made its mark as Singapore’s affordable premium gym brand, serving
the local water sports community. With more than 3 gyms (and counting) island-wide, PaddleShed will
continue to expand and bring quality gym facilities and service to the rest of Singapore and beyond.
