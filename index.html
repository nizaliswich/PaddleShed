Facilities

THEGYM

Get fit and have fun with our top-of-the-line gym
equipment. We offer the latest and best in exercise
equipment and a friendly environment where our
friendly staff are always available to provide assistance
and guidance.

THEERGO

Don't let the category one blues stop you from your
training. Head on down to one of our gyms to use our
ergometer room. We have ergometers that caters to the
kayakers, canoeist, rowers and dragonboaters.

THECAFE

Once you are done with your training, head on down to the
cafe to refuel. The perfect place to end your training.
Not to mention the best place to get to know the athletes
from the same field.
